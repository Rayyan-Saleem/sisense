At Sipsense, we go the extra mile to ensure that the water you drink is as pure as it can get. We have a 10 step quality insurance procedure to treat the water before it is bottled. The bottles are sterlised before each and every time it is refilled. And we listen to our customers all the time to better our services. So when we tell you that Sipsense is pure, it is our guarantee.

At Sipsense, we give as much importance to on-time delivery of water as to our purity. Hence we have invested in quality assurance, which means we have hired well-trained, cheerful staff to ensure that you get timely and a pleasant delivery experience. You can also order water online by calling to our customer support representatives.

The SipSense sustainability journey is focused on 3 pillars:

Packaging and recycling

Packaging allows us to bring our water to you, our consumer, preserving the natural spring water’s quality–but we believe it shouldn’t come at the expense of the environment. After all, our water couldn’t exist without a healthy environment.

Promoting Health and Nutrition

We aim to make safe, tasty and sustainable water that is nutritious, accessible and affordable to everyone.

Protecting Nature

Water is key to all life and deeply connected to regeneration. We rely on water and recognize our role in helping to ensure access to sufficient clean supplies.
